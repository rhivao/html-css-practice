All About Fruits

Fruits are natural foods that grow on plants and trees. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Eating fruits daily helps us stay healthy and energetic.

Types of Fruits

1. Tropical Fruits

These fruits grow in warm climates.

  • Mango
  • Pineapple
  • Banana
  • Papaya

2. Citrus Fruits

These fruits are rich in Vitamin C.

  • Orange
  • Lemon
  • Sweet Lime
  • Grapefruit

3. Dry Fruits

Dry fruits are nutritious and good for health.

  1. Almond
  2. Cashew
  3. Walnut
  4. Raisin

Benefits of Eating Fruits

  • Improve digestion
  • Boost immunity
  • Provide natural energy
  • Keep skin healthy

Conclusion: Fruits are an important part of a balanced diet. We should include different types of fruits in our daily meals.
