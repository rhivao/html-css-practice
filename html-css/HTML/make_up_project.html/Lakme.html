Lakmé Cosmetics

Brief introduction about Lakmé Cosmetics:

Lakmé is a leading Indian beauty brand offering high-performance cosmetics, skincare, and salon services tailored to Indian skin tones. It was the first cosmetic brand to introduce makeup to Indian women and has been a beauty expert for over 50 years. Lakmé also connects beauty with fashion through Lakmé Fashion Week, one of India’s largest fashion events.

(Source: Lakmé Cosmetics )
